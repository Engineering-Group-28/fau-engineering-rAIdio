About FAU Engineering rAIdio Station

Our Mission

The FAU Engineering rAIdio Station is an AI-powered web radio designed to serve the Florida Atlantic University Engineering community. Our project combines artificial intelligence, web scraping, and generative audio to automatically collect, summarize, and broadcast FAU-related news, research, and events through a podcast-style stream.

Why We Created This Project

Audio-based platforms like radio and podcasts are a valuable way to share information, entertainment, and knowledge â€” especially when multitasking. However, traditional university news sources can be scattered across multiple sites and social feeds. Our goal was to create a centralized, engaging, and fully automated solution that informs students and faculty through AI-generated audio segments.
